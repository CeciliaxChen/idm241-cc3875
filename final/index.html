Dyslexia Main Tab

Triggers

ON HOVER

  • hover on the "Dyslexia" main tab on the menu

OFF HOVER

  • hover off the "Dyslexia" main tab on the menu

Rules

ON HOVER

  • the thin, turqoise underline on the tab would start from the left and travel horizontally to the end of the word
  • a turquoise box will travel from the left to fill the background of the selected font sub category to the right until it is completely filled
  • the sub categories will consequentially ease in 0.35s in, one sub category after another from top to bottom
  • the arrow curser turns into the pointer curser
  • as the box gets filled from left to right, the letters change from black to purple

OFF HOVER

  • the thin, turqoise underline on the tab would start to horizontally retract from the right tab and travel to the start of the word
  • a turquoise box will travel from the left to unfill the background of the selected font sub category to the right until the background is completely back to white
  • the sub categories will consequentially ease out 0.35s in, one sub category after another from bottom to top to bottom
  • the pointer curser turns into the arrow curser
  • as the box gets unfilled from left to right, the letters change from purple back to black

Feedback

ON HOVER

  • a turquoise box will travel from the left to fill the background of the selected font sub category to the right until it is completely filled
  • the sub categories will consequentially ease in 0.35s in, one sub category after another from top to bottom
  • the arrow curser turns into the pointer curser
  • as the box gets filled from left to right, the letters change from black to purple

OFF HOVER

  • the thin, turqoise underline on the tab would start to horizontally retract from the right tab and travel to the start of the word
  • a turquoise box will travel from the left to unfill the background of the selected font sub category to the right until the background is completely back to white
  • the sub categories will consequentially ease out 0.35s in, one sub category after another from bottom to top to bottom
  • the pointer curser turns into the arrow curser
  • as the box gets unfilled from left to right, the letters change from purple back to black

Loops and Modes

  • sub categories of menu appear on hover and sub categories of menu disappear off hover

Sub Category Fonts

Triggers

ON CLICK

  • click on the sub categories of fonts after hovering on the 'OpenDyslexic' tab

OFF CLICK

  • click off the sub categories of fonts after hovering on the 'OpenDyslexic' tab

Rules

  • the word of the font is bolded when clicked on
  • the word of the font is unbolded when another font is selected

Feedback

  • the word of the font is bolded
  • the word of the font is unbolded when another font is selected

Loops and Modes

  • none
