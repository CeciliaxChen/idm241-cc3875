Dyslexia Main Tab

Triggers

  • hover on and off the "Dyslexia" main tab on the menu
  • hover on and off the sub categories tabs in the menu
  • click on and off the sub categories of fonts after hovering on the 'Dyslexia' tab
  • hover on and off the "RESET" button below the sample text
  • click on and off the "RESET" button below the sample text

Rules

  • the thin, turqoise underline on the tab would start from the left and travel horizontally to the end of the word on hover and the underline will retract off hover
  • the thin, turqoise underline on the tab would retract horizontally off hover
  • a turquoise box will travel from the left to fill the background of the selected font sub category to the right until it is completely filled on hover
  • a turquoise box will travel unfill from the selected font sub category off hover
  • the sub categories will consequentially ease in 0.35s on hover and ease out 0.35s off hover, one sub category after another from top to bottom
  • the arrow curser turns into the pointer curser on hover and turns back to the arrow off hover
  • as the box gets filled from left to right, the letters change from black to purple on hover
  • as the box gets unfilled from left to right, the letters change from purple back to black
  • the word of the font is animated to enlarge by 50% when clicked
  • the word of the font reverts back to its original size when another font is selected
  • the sample text of the different fonts will change based on the font being selected on
