Dyslexia Main Tab

Triggers

ON HOVER

  • hover on the "Dyslexia" main tab on the menu

OFF HOVER

  • hover off the "Dyslexia" main tab on the menu

Rules

ON HOVER

  • a turquoise box will travel from the left to fill the background of the selected font sub category to the right until it is completely filled
  • as the box gets filled from left to right, the letters change from black to purple

OFF HOVER

  • a turquoise box will travel from the left to unfill the background of the selected font sub category to the right until the background is completely back to white
  • as the box gets unfilled from left to right, the letters change from purple back to black

Feedback

ON HOVER

  • a turquoise box will travel from the left to fill the background of the selected font sub category to the right until it is completely filled
  • as the box gets filled from left to right, the letters change from black to purple

OFF HOVER

  • a turquoise box will travel from the left to unfill the background of the selected font sub category to the right until the background is completely back to white
  • as the box gets unfilled from left to right, the letters change from purple back to black

Loops and Modes

  • none

Sub Category Fonts

Triggers

ON CLICK

  • click on the sub categories of fonts after hovering on the Dyslexia tab, specifically the 'OpenDyslexic' tab

OFF CLICK

  • click off the sub categories of fonts after hovering on the Dyslexia tab, specifically the 'OpenDyslexic' tab

Rules

ON CLICK

  • two horizontal lines fade in from above and below the word of the font being selected on
  • the two horizontal lines hug the word of the font

OFF CLICK

  • two horizontal lines fade out from above and below the word of the font being selected on
  • the two horizontal lines retract from the word of the font into the on-hover state

Feedback

ON CLICK

  • two horizontal lines fade in from above and below the word of the font being selected on
  • the two horizontal lines hug the word of the font

OFF CLICK

  • two horizontal lines fade out from above and below the word of the font being selected on
  • the two horizontal lines retract from the word of the font into the on-hover state

Loops and Modes

  • none
