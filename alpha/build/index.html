Triggers

Rules

ON HOVER

  • the thin, orange underline on the tab would start from the left and travel horizontally to the end of the word
  • the sub categories will consequentially ease in 0.35 second in, one sub category after another from top to bottom
  • the arrow curser turns into the pointer curser

OFF HOVER

  • the thin, orange underline on the tab would start to horizontally retract from the right tab and travel to the start of the word
  • the sub categories will consequentially ease out 0.35 second in, one sub category after another from bottom to top to bottom
  • the pointer curser turns into the arrow curser
